# Fitness for Programmers

Physical health directly affects cognitive output.

## Minimal routine

- Strength training 3 times per week
- Daily walking target
- Short mobility session

## Workday habits

- Stand every 50 minutes
- Hydrate consistently
- Take eye breaks

Consistency is more valuable than intensity.
