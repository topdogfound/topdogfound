# Gaming in Moderation for High Performers

Gaming can be recovery or distraction. Boundaries decide.

## Healthy framework

- Pre-decide session length
- Avoid late-night ranked queues
- Play after meaningful work

## Transferable skills

- Resource allocation
- Adaptation under uncertainty
- Team communication

Play intentionally, not reactively.
