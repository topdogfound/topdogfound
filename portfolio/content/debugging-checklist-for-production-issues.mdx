# A Debugging Checklist for Production Issues

Incidents require process, not heroics.

## First 15 minutes

- Confirm blast radius
- Freeze risky deploys
- Capture recent changes

## Investigation sequence

1. Reproduce
2. Narrow scope
3. Validate assumptions with metrics/logs
4. Roll back or patch safely

Always follow with a blameless postmortem.
