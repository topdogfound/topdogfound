# How to Think Clearly in Chaotic Times

Clarity is an advantage when noise is high.

## Decision framework

- Separate facts from narratives
- Focus on controllables
- Write reasoning for major choices
- Delay decisions in emotional spikes

Calm thinking improves both career and life outcomes.
