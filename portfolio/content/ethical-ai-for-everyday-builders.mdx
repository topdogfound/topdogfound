# Ethical AI for Everyday Builders

Ethics becomes practical when mapped to release checks.

## Minimum checks

- Document purpose and limitations
- Add fallback for high-risk decisions
- Monitor quality by user segment
- Explain outcomes when feasible

Trust is a product feature, not marketing text.
