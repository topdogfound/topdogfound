# Geopolitics and Software Supply Chains

Global events now directly impact engineering planning.

## Practical effects

- Hardware and cloud pricing volatility
- Data residency constraints
- Vendor concentration risk

## Developer checklist

- Avoid single-region critical dependencies
- Maintain vendor fallback options
- Document compliance assumptions

This is operational risk management, not speculation.
