# India Tech Policy Signals Developers Should Track

Policy literacy is now a product and engineering skill.

## Areas to monitor

- Data protection requirements
- Platform governance rules
- Fintech and payment compliance
- Cross-border data flow constraints

## Team action

- Review compliance assumptions quarterly
- Involve legal early on data-heavy features
- Keep data flow maps updated

Preventive compliance beats emergency rework.
